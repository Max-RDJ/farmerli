1. Choose Your Plan

Select a subscription that fits your household — from small weekly fruit boxes to larger mixed produce deliveries for families.

3. We Sort & Prepare Deliveries

Produce is carefully sorted, packed, and prepared for delivery while keeping waste and excess packaging to a minimum.

4. Delivered to Your Door

Your produce arrives fresh each week through flexible local delivery routes designed to reduce environmental impact.

5. Cook, Share & Enjoy

Discover seasonal ingredients, cook more sustainably, and enjoy fresh produce while supporting local agriculture.

6. Reduce Waste Every Week

Every subscription helps rescue edible food from being discarded, reducing waste while helping farms earn more from their harvests.

Why It Matters

Traditional retail systems reject huge amounts of perfectly edible produce every year. Farmerli helps redirect that food into homes instead of landfill.
